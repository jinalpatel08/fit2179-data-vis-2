Australia's Changing Climate

How rising temperatures, shifting rainfall, and worsening extremes are reshaping the continent — 1910 to today

Australia is heating up

Since 1910, Australia's average temperature has risen by approximately 1.5°C. The warming has accelerated since the 1950s, with the most recent decade being the hottest on record.
